Kitchen Smart

With Smart Home technology based on Android, the kitchen cupboard door becomes interactive.
The Kitchen Smart conjures up cooking videos,
Internet TV and many other attractive apps on its smart surface at the touch of a finger or via voice control.
Via Bluetooth or WiFi, it provides a connection to smart kitchen appliances and consumer electronics.
Its built-in camera allows you to Skype and chat, while the integrated microphone offers perfect voice control.
Developed specifically for the compact oven cabinet, its high-quality glass surface adapts perfectly to common kitchen appliances.
The storage space is retained, and the display function is invisible when switched off.
