About Us

Excellent entertainment and smart home electronics are only as good as their technology. We at Mues-Tec don't want to leave anything to chance. That's why we are committed to Germany as a business location twice. We develop our products at our company headquarters near Fulda and many devices from our portfolio are manufactured in Germany. In dialogue with customers from industry and trade, our engineers and product designers work hand in hand to develop high-quality, marketable devices. All quality-critical steps take place at the company headquarters. Mues-Tec works with renowned planning and architectural offices throughout Europe.
